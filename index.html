Our Story Who We Are

Born from a passion for exceptional fragrance

Lamoh Perfumes was founded with a single belief: that everyone deserves access to truly extraordinary scents. What started as a personal obsession with rare and captivating fragrances grew into something much greater — a curated destination for perfume lovers across the UAE.

We source our collection from the world's finest fragrance houses, selecting only those bottles that tell a genuine story — ones that transport you somewhere, awaken something, or simply make you feel undeniably yourself.

Whether you seek a bold oud-forward statement, a delicate floral whisper, or a modern woody signature, our collection has been assembled with your experience in mind.

Shop Policy Legal & Privacy

1. General Terms

By placing an order with Lamoh Perfumes, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. We reserve the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the current policy.

2. Authenticity Guarantee

Every fragrance sold by Lamoh Perfumes is 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors. We do not sell counterfeit, tester, or tampered products. If you have any doubts, contact us immediately.

3. Orders & Payment

Orders are placed via Instagram DM or WhatsApp. We accept the following payment methods:

  • Bank Transfer (UAE accounts)
  • Cash on Delivery (selected areas)
  • PayPal / Wise (on request)

4. Shipping & Delivery

We deliver across the UAE. Timeframes:

  • Dubai: 1–2 business days
  • Abu Dhabi & Sharjah: 2–3 business days
  • Other Emirates: 3–5 business days

5. Returns & Exchanges

Due to the nature of perfume products, we do not accept returns on opened items unless the product arrived damaged or is inauthentic. Unopened items may be exchanged within 3 days of delivery, subject to availability.

6. Privacy Policy

Lamoh Perfumes respects your privacy. Any personal information (name, phone, address) is used solely to process and deliver your order. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties.

7. Contact

For any questions, reach out via Instagram @lamohperfumes or WhatsApp +971 55 283 6936.
